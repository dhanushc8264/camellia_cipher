Welcome to Camellia Cipher

The Camellia cipher is a modern, symmetric key block cipher with a block size of 128 bits and key sizes of 128, 192, and 256 bits.

Developed by Mitsubishi Electric and NTT of Japan, Camellia is approved by the ISO/IEC, NESSIE, and CRYPTREC projects for its high level of security and performance.

Explore the various sections to learn more about its usage, algorithm, implementation, and security features.
